# Trust Signals

Trust is often a UI problem. Users need signals that the system is correct, stable, and reversible.

Examples of trust signals:
- Identity confirmation (photo/name/secondary identifiers)
- Clear success + failure states
- Reversibility (undo, edit, change)
- Predictable motion and feedback

Use trust signals aggressively in high-stakes flows, especially alongside [[studio.design.patterns.premium-interaction-patterns]].
